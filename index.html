Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Mira is built privacy-first. Your cycle data belongs to you, and we designed the app so that we never have to be trusted with it.

What we store, and where

All cycle, symptom, mood, and note data is stored locally on your device by default. Nothing is sent to any server unless you choose to turn on optional cloud sync.

Optional encrypted cloud sync

If you enable cloud sync, your data is encrypted on your device with a key that only you hold (your recovery key) before it ever leaves your phone. Our servers store only unreadable ciphertext — we cannot read your data, and we cannot recover it without your recovery key. Sign-in uses your email address and a one-time code.

What we do not do

No analytics SDKs. No advertising. No third-party trackers. We never sell or share your data with anyone. Mira contains no hidden data collection.

Your controls

You can export all of your data (CSV or PDF) at any time, and permanently delete everything from Settings → Delete all data. Deleting removes it from your device and, if cloud sync is enabled, from our encrypted store.

Subscriptions

Optional premium features are handled by the App Store / Google Play and RevenueCat. Purchase processing is performed by the stores; we do not receive your payment card details.

Children

Mira is intended for adults and is not directed at children under 13.

Changes

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Material changes will be reflected here with an updated date.
